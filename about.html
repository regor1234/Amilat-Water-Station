About Us

Amilat Water Refilling Station has been serving the community since February 22, 2020. We ensure a safe, drinkable, healthy, and reliable water source! The idea for Amilat Water Refilling Station started when my mother was planning to start a business. Her job alone couldn't cover our school tuition fees, so she decided to establish a water refilling station.

At first, she purchased a plot of land, and after a few months, the water station was built. The official opening of Amilat Water Station took place on February 22, 2020, at San Lorenzo Tinio Street, Gapan City, Nueva Ecija.

We come from Angeles, Pampanga, but we chose to start our water business in Gapan City, Nueva Ecija. Since my family members live in Gapan, it was the perfect location. We hired staff so we could travel back to Angeles whenever needed.
